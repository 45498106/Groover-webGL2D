Groover-WebGL2D
Fullscreen grid. webGL1 shader.

When displaying grids, rendering the background grid can have a significant performance hit. This shader can overcome this problem by using the power of the GPU to render the grid for you in real-time.

Click and drag to pan the grid. Use mouse wheel to zoom in and out. Select a style from the buttons below.

You have control over a variety of options.
  • The 2 checker colours.
  • Grid colour.
  • Grid line width.
  • How quickly different grid levels fade in and out.
  • Grid line spacing, from 2 to 16.
  • Grid line detail level 2 or 3 levels.
  • Turn on or off grid lines and or checker pattern.
